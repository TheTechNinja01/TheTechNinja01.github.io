Smart FD is a Durban based visionary financial management firm here to serve start-up enterprises and long-serving owner-managed companies. We currently operate in the greater Durban area with specific clients in the rest of South Africa.

The financial function is at the heart of every business. Sound financial planning and management enable businesses to grow and flourish, while the lack thereof is always a fundamental reason for failure. Being entrepreneurs ourselves, we understand the significant role that finance plays in the success of a business.

Certain SME companies cannot afford to appoint directors and managers to correctly steer their business in the right direction. Smart FD offers the solution of being able to correctly navigate your business towards success without the burden of exorbitant costs.

We offer a personalized service tailor-made to suit your business. We have broad experience in all aspects of accounting, auditing, tax and financial management.

We are always looking for opportunities for our firm to help your business from a financial perspective or to network with our current clients to improve your growth.
